SPESIFIKASI ALAT BERAT KONSTRUKSI

I. ALAT BERAT PENGGALIAN

1. Excavator
Hydraulic Excavator 20-25 Ton:
- Engine Power: 110-130 HP
- Bucket Capacity: 1.0-1.2 m³
- Operating Weight: 20,000-25,000 kg
- Digging Depth: 6.5-7.0 m
- Digging Reach: 9.5-10.0 m
- Swing Speed: 11-13 RPM
- Travel Speed: 4.5-5.5 km/h
- Fuel Tank: 300-400 L
- Hydraulic System: Load sensing

Mini Excavator 5-8 Ton:
- Engine Power: 35-55 HP
- Bucket Capacity: 0.25-0.35 m³
- Operating Weight: 5,000-8,000 kg
- Digging Depth: 3.5-4.5 m
- Zero tail swing untuk area sempit
- Rubber track untuk permukaan halus

2. Wheel Loader
Medium Wheel Loader:
- Engine Power: 150-200 HP
- Bucket Capacity: 2.5-3.5 m³
- Operating Weight: 12,000-16,000 kg
- Breakout Force: 110-150 kN
- Dump Height: 2.8-3.2 m
- Travel Speed: 40-45 km/h
- Fuel Tank: 200-300 L
- Tire Size: 20.5-25 atau 23.5-25

3. Bulldozer
Medium Bulldozer:
- Engine Power: 130-180 HP
- Operating Weight: 15,000-22,000 kg
- Blade Capacity: 3.5-5.0 m³
- Ground Pressure: 55-75 kPa
- Travel Speed: 10-12 km/h forward
- Drawbar Pull: 140-200 kN
- Track Width: 560-610 mm
- Blade Type: Straight, Semi-U, U

VI. SPESIFIKASI TEKNIS UMUM

1. Engine Requirements
- Emission Standard: EURO III minimum
- Fuel Type: Solar/Diesel
- Cooling System: Water cooled
- Air Filter: Dry type dengan pre-cleaner
- Oil Change Interval: 250-500 jam
- Fuel Consumption sesuai standar

2. Hydraulic System
- System Type: Load sensing
- Pump Type: Variable displacement
- Working Pressure: 250-350 bar
- Filtration: 10-25 micron
- Oil Temperature: -20°C hingga +80°C
- Quick Coupler untuk attachment

3. Operator Cab
- ROPS/FOPS certified cab
- Air conditioning system
- Ergonomic seat dengan suspension
- Low noise level < 80 dB(A)
- Visibility sesuai standar ISO 5006
- Instrumentation panel lengkap

VII. MAINTENANCE DAN SERVICE

1. Preventive Maintenance
- Daily check: Oli, air, bahan bakar
- 50 jam: Greasing dan basic check
- 250 jam: Engine oil dan filter change
- 500 jam: Hydraulic oil dan filter
- 1000 jam: Major service
- 2000 jam: Engine overhaul

2. Service Support
- Authorized dealer network
- Genuine spare parts availability
- Technical training untuk operator
- Emergency service 24/7
- Warranty period minimum 1 tahun
- Service manual dalam bahasa Indonesia

VIII. SAFETY FEATURES

1. Standard Safety Equipment
- ROPS (Roll Over Protective Structure)
- FOPS (Falling Object Protective Structure)
- Seat belt dan emergency stop
- Warning lights dan alarm
- Fire extinguisher
- First aid kit

2. Operational Safety
- Load moment indicator (untuk crane)
- Overload protection system
- Auto-leveling system (untuk concrete pump)
- Backup alarm dan rotating beacon
- Non-slip steps dan handrails
- Emergency escape routes
