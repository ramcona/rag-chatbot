STANDAR OPERASIONAL PROSEDUR (SOP)
QUALITY CONTROL KONSTRUKSI

I. SISTEM MANAJEMEN MUTU

1. Organisasi Quality Control
- QC Manager: bertanggung jawab keseluruhan
- QC Inspector: inspeksi harian pekerjaan
- QC Tester: pengujian material dan hasil kerja
- QC Administrator: dokumentasi dan laporan

2. Dokumentasi Mutu
- Quality Manual dan prosedur
- Inspection and Test Plan (ITP)
- Quality Control Forms dan checklist
- Non-Conformance Report (NCR)

II. INSPEKSI PEKERJAAN STRUKTUR

1. Pekerjaan Pondasi
- Survey dan setting out
- Penggalian dan leveling
- Pemasangan bekisting dan tulangan
- Pengecoran dan curing beton
- Hold Point: sebelum pengecoran

2. Pekerjaan Struktur Atas
- Fabrikasi dan erection tulangan
- Kualitas bekisting dan alignment
- Concrete pouring procedure
- Compressive strength test
- Hold Point: tulangan, bekisting, pengecoran

3. Pekerjaan Baja Struktural
- Shop drawing approval
- Material certification review
- Welding procedure qualification
- Dimensional inspection
- NDT testing untuk sambungan

III. PENGUJIAN MATERIAL

1. Pengujian Beton
Frequency Testing:
- Slump test: setiap truck mixer
- Cube/cylinder test: 1 set per 50 m³
- Core drilling: jika hasil test tidak memenuhi
- Non-destructive test: rebound hammer

Acceptance Criteria:
- Individual test ≥ 0.85 x fck
- Average 4 consecutive tests ≥ fck + 4 MPa
- No single test < fck - 4 MPa

2. Pengujian Baja Tulangan
- Tensile test: 2 samples per size per 10 ton
- Bend test: 2 samples per size per 10 ton
- Chemical analysis: 1 per heat number
- Visual inspection: 100% untuk crack, rust

3. Pengujian Agregat
- Gradasi: setiap 200 ton atau source change
- Specific gravity dan absorption
- Los Angeles abrasion test
- Alkali-silica reactivity test

IV. INSPEKSI PEKERJAAN FINISHING

1. Pekerjaan Dinding
- Verticality check dengan waterpass
- Surface flatness ± 3 mm per 2 m
- Mortar joint consistency
- Crack inspection dan repair

2. Pekerjaan Lantai
- Level accuracy ± 3 mm per 3 m
- Surface hardness test
- Water absorption test untuk keramik
- Joint alignment dan grouting quality

3. Pekerjaan Cat
- Surface preparation standard
- Primer application dan coverage
- Finish coat thickness measurement
- Color matching approval
- Defect inspection: chalking, peeling, cracking

V. NON-CONFORMANCE MANAGEMENT

1. NCR Process
- Identification dan documentation
- Root cause analysis
- Corrective action plan
- Implementation dan verification
- Closure approval

2. Rework Procedure
- Work suspension notice
- Rework method statement
- Re-inspection protocol
- Additional testing requirements
- Cost impact assessment

VI. QUALITY ASSURANCE AUDIT

1. Internal Audit
- Monthly QC system audit
- Random work inspection
- Documentation review
- Training needs assessment

2. Third Party Inspection
- Independent quality auditor
- Client representative inspection
- Regulatory body inspection
- International standard compliance

VII. QUALITY METRICS DAN KPI

1. Quality Performance Indicators
- First Time Right Rate > 90%
- Rework Cost < 3% of project value
- NCR Close-out Time < 7 days
- Customer Satisfaction Score > 8/10

2. Quality Reporting
- Daily QC inspection report
- Weekly quality dashboard
- Monthly quality metrics
- Project quality summary report
